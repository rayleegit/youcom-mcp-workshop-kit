# Lab 3: Research Agent Brief

## Goal

Use a deeper research workflow to produce a short, decision-ready brief.

## Prompt

```text
Research how product teams should evaluate citation quality in AI search experiences.
Return a one-page brief with risks, useful metrics, and a launch checklist.
```

## Debrief

- What did the assistant do well?
- Where did the evidence feel thin?
- What would you turn into an automated eval?
